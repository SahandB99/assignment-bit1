Leonardo DiCaprio

about the Actor

Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio (born November 11, 1974) is an American actor and film producer. Known for his work as a leading man in biopics and period films, he is the recipient of numerous accolades, including an Academy Award, a British Academy Film Award, and three Golden Globe Awards.
